About Savoiré AI

Savoiré AI is a free study assistant built by Sooban Talha Technologies. It turns any topic into notes, flashcards, quizzes, summaries and mind maps — instantly, in more than 20 languages.

Version 2.0 was built around one idea: studying should feel like a conversation with a brilliant tutor, not a fight with a blank page. Every tool streams its output live, so you watch your material take shape instead of waiting on it.

Savoiré AI is developed and maintained by Sooban Talha Technologies. Learn more at soobantalhatech.xyz.

Privacy

Savoiré AI does not require an account and does not sell your data. Topics you submit are used only to generate the study material you request.

We keep the minimum data necessary to make the product work — things like your generated content history stored locally in your browser, and basic anonymized usage metrics to improve reliability.

We never share your study topics or generated content with advertisers. If you have questions about your data, reach out through our GitHub or developer site.
