FAQ

Common Questions

Is Savoiré AI really completely free?
Yes — 100% free, permanently. No hidden fees, no trial period, no premium tier, no credit card ever required. Every tool, every feature, every export is free for everyone, forever.
Do I need an account or login?
No. You simply enter your name on your first visit. No email, no password. Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage — completely private.
Why does generation sometimes take 20–60 seconds?
Free AI models can be busy during peak hours. Savoiré AI automatically tries 10 different models in sequence with no timeout — it will ALWAYS deliver a result, just wait and it will come. We never cut you off.
What AI powers Savoiré AI?
Savoiré AI uses 10 advanced language models including Gemini, LLaMA, DeepSeek, and others via a smart fallback chain. If one model is busy, the next takes over automatically. You always see "Powered by Savoiré AI."
Can I export my notes as PDF?
Yes! Every output can be exported as a beautifully formatted PDF with Savoiré AI branding, page numbers, and professional layout. Just click "Download PDF."
Which languages are supported?
Over 50 languages including English, Urdu, Hindi, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, Turkish, Bengali and many more.
Who built Savoiré AI?
Savoiré AI was built by Sooban Talha of Sooban Talha Technologies with the mission of making world-class AI study tools free for every student everywhere. Visit savoireai.vercel.app.
