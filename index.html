## Quantum Entanglement
Quantum entanglement is one of the most fascinating and counterintuitive phenomena in all of physics. When two particles interact in certain ways, they become permanently linked — measuring one instantly determines the state of the other, regardless of the distance between them.
## Key Concepts
Bell's Theorem — Proves that quantum correlations cannot be explained by classical hidden variable theories. John Bell (1964) derived inequalities that any local realistic theory must satisfy.
EPR Paradox — Einstein, Podolsky and Rosen challenged quantum mechanics in 1935, arguing it was "incomplete." Their thought experiment prompted decades of groundbreaking experimental work.
## Real-World Applications
Quantum cryptography uses entanglement to create theoretically unbreakable encryption keys. Quantum computing leverages entangled qubits to perform calculations exponentially faster than classical computers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is Savoiré AI really 100% free — forever?
Yes, completely and permanently free. There are no hidden fees, no paywalls, no premium tiers, no credit card required at any point. Savoiré AI is built on OpenRouter's free-tier AI models and is committed to staying 100% free forever. Every feature — live streaming, PDF export, quizzes, flashcards, mind maps, 50+ languages, history, saved notes — is available to every student everywhere at no cost.
How does the live streaming output work?
Savoiré AI uses Server-Sent Events (SSE) — the same technology used by ChatGPT, Claude and Gemini — to stream the AI's response token-by-token directly to your browser the moment it is generated. You see each word appear on screen in real time with no waiting. The streaming typically begins within 1–2 seconds of clicking Generate. On mobile devices, the screen automatically scrolls to the output area so you can watch the live generation without doing anything.
What if the AI is slow or unavailable?
Savoiré AI has 10 free AI models with automatic failover. If the primary model is busy or slow, the system tries the next model automatically — up to 2 attempts per model, 10 models total, for a maximum of 20 attempts. Rate-limited models are skipped immediately. In the extremely rare event all 10 models are unavailable simultaneously, a high-quality offline fallback generates complete study content from your topic locally — and it even streams word-by-word so you still see live output.
Is my data private? Is it stored anywhere?
Your study history, saved notes and preferences are stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — they never leave your device and are never sent to any server. The only data sent to the server is your topic text to generate study content. We do not collect, log, store or sell your personal data or study history. You can export all your data as a JSON file at any time, or delete everything with one click from Settings.
Can I use it on my phone?
Absolutely. Savoiré AI is fully responsive and optimised for all devices — smartphones, tablets, laptops and desktops. On mobile, when you tap Generate the screen automatically scrolls to the output area so you can watch the live streaming output without doing anything. The interface adapts to any screen size with proper touch targets, readable typography and a clean mobile layout. The flashcards, quiz and all interactive features work perfectly on touch screens.
What subjects and topics does it cover?
Every subject at every level — from GCSE to PhD. Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Maths), Humanities (History, Geography, Literature, Philosophy), Social Sciences (Economics, Psychology, Sociology, Law), Technology (Computer Science, AI, Programming), Business (Finance, Marketing, Management, Accounting), Medicine, Engineering, Arts, Languages, and much more. You can also paste your own text — articles, textbook chapters, lecture notes — and have Savoiré AI generate study materials from it.
How do I deploy my own copy?
Deploy to Vercel in under 5 minutes: (1) Upload the project files. (2) Place api/study.js at that path. (3) Add one environment variable: OPENROUTER_API_KEY — get it free at openrouter.ai. (4) Set maxDuration: 300 in vercel.json. That's it — all 10 free AI models work with a free OpenRouter account, no credit card needed.
