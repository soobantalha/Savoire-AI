Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Savoiré AI really free? +
Yes, completely. No credit card, no login, no payment wall — ever. Savoiré AI is built on free-tier AI models via OpenRouter, which means the cost of running it is zero, and that saving passes directly to you.
What's "Live Streaming" and why does it matter? +
Instead of waiting for the AI to finish generating a full response, Savoiré AI sends each token to your screen as it's produced. This means text appears within ~1 second and you start reading immediately — exactly like ChatGPT or Claude.
What if the AI fails or is busy? +
Savoiré AI uses 10 AI models in sequence with smart failover. If the first model is unavailable or rate-limited, it automatically tries the next — and the next — until it finds one that works. There's also a high-quality offline fallback that streams word by word.
How many languages are supported? +
Over 50 languages, including English, Urdu, Arabic, French, Spanish, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, and many more. The output is generated entirely in your selected language, not just translated after the fact.
Can I export my notes? +
Yes. Every result can be exported as a professionally formatted multi-page A4 PDF (powered by jsPDF), copied to clipboard, or shared via the Web Share API. You can also save notes to your personal library and access them later.
Does it require a login? +
No. You enter your name on first visit and that's it. All your history and saved notes are stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent to a server or requires account creation.
What writing styles and depths are available? +
Four depth levels: Standard (600–900 words), Detailed (1,000–1,500 words), Comprehensive (1,500–2,000 words), and Expert (2,000–2,800 words). Multiple writing styles including academic, casual, and storytelling are also available.
Who built Savoiré AI? +
Savoiré AI was designed and built by Sooban Talha, founder of Sooban Talha Technologies. The mission is simple: remove every barrier between a student and quality learning. Free for every student on Earth, forever.
