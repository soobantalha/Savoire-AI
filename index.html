✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Savoiré AI really 100% free?
Yes, completely. There are no hidden fees, no paywalls, no credit card required. Savoiré AI is powered by OpenRouter's free-tier AI models and is committed to staying free forever. Every single feature — streaming, PDF, quizzes, flashcards, 50+ languages — is available to everyone.
How does the live streaming work?
Savoiré AI uses Server-Sent Events (SSE) to stream the AI's response token-by-token directly to your browser. This means you see each word appear in real time as the AI generates it — no waiting for a full response. The streaming starts typically within 1-3 seconds of hitting Generate.
What if the AI fails or is slow?
Savoiré AI has 20 free AI models with automatic failover. If the primary model is slow or unavailable, the system tries the next one automatically — using circuit breaker logic and exponential backoff. In the extremely unlikely event all 20 models fail, the system generates offline fallback content so you always get something useful.
Is my data stored or shared?
Your study history and preferences are stored locally in your browser's localStorage — they never leave your device. We don't collect, store, or share your notes or personal data. The only data sent to the server is your topic query to generate the notes.
Can I use it on mobile?
Absolutely. Savoiré AI is fully responsive and optimised for all screen sizes — smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. The interface adapts beautifully to any device with touch-friendly controls, proper tap targets, and a clean mobile layout.
