Frequently Asked Questions

Is Savoiré AI really completely free? ▼

Yes. Completely, permanently, 100% free. No hidden charges. No trial periods. No premium tier. Every tool, every feature, available to everyone forever.

Do I need to create an account or log in? ▼

No account. No login. No email. On first visit you simply enter your name — that's it. Everything is stored privately in your browser.

What happens to my notes and data? ▼

Everything you create stays in YOUR browser via localStorage. Nothing is ever sent to or stored on our servers. Your data is 100% private.

What AI powers Savoiré? ▼

Savoiré AI uses a proprietary ensemble of advanced language models, continuously updated with the latest AI research. We handle all the complexity — you just get results.

Which file types can I upload? ▼

PDF (.pdf), Word (.docx), Plain text (.txt), Images (.png, .jpg, .webp) with OCR text recognition.

How many tools are available? ▼

Over 160 tools across 10 categories: notes, summarization, meetings, study, research, content, documents, rewriting, and more.

Which languages are supported? ▼

Over 50 languages including all major European, Asian, Middle Eastern, and South Asian languages.

Can I export my notes as PDF? ▼

Yes. Export any output as PDF, Markdown, or plain text with one click — fully formatted, branded with Savoiré AI.

Does it work on mobile? ▼

Perfectly. Savoiré AI is fully responsive and optimized for mobile browsers.
